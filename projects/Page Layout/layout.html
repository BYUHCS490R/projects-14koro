Air Jordan 1 "Chicago"

I really love sneakers. Out of the roughly 14 pairs I own, 5 are Nike shoes. When creating this website, I wanted to share the appeal of Nike sneakers. Nike has several popular shoes that are loved by a wide range of generations. Next time you're walking around town, take a look at people's feet. You might just spot some!

This page features the most famous colorway in the Nike Air Jordan 1 series: the Chicago. It's said the Chicago name comes from the team colors of the Chicago Bulls, the famous basketball player Michael Jordan played for at the time. When this colorway launched in the 1980s, bright colors were in style, making it incredibly popular. Its popularity continues to this day.

Family Members

Grandmother

My grandmother is my mother's mother. She turned 95 this year. She is the kindest grandmother ever to us grandchildren and great-grandchildren!

Parents

Both my parents are 72 years old. My father worked to support our family of ten on his own. My mother raised us all to be healthy on her own. They will soon celebrate their 40th wedding anniversary.

Siblings

My oldest brother is 41, and my youngest brother is 24, so there's a 17-year age gap. We have many siblings with large age differences, but we all get along great! Six out of ten are married, and they have many children too.

Grandchildren

From my perspective, they are my nephews and nieces. I now have a total of 22 nieces and nephews, and the youngest was just born last month! Spending time with them is one of my favorite things to do.

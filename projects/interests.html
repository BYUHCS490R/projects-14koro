Why I Love Sneakers

I currently own fourteen pairs of sneakers. I am a sneakerhead. There are numerous brands currently dealing in sneakers, with famous ones including NIKE, CONVERSE, and ADIDAS. Around 1980, NIKE's Air Jordan series ignited the popularity of sneakers and is still loved by many fans today.

Sneakers are my identity. I love dressing up, and I'm especially particular about choosing my sneakers. Even if I'm wearing ordinary clothes, as long as I'm wearing cool sneakers, I can enjoy the whole day.

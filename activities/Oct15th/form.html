The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The action attribute specifies where the information entered via the form should be submitted.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

The method attribute specifies how the submitted data is sent.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

Get displays the entered information in the URL, but Post does not.

Question

When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

You can use background image when you want to decolate a website. Not have to.

The IMG tag is part of the content, while the background is part of the decoration. Therefore, the IMG tag should be used when you want to emphasize that image or convey that it is relevant to the website's subject matter.

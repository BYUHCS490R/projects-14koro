Weekly Fitness Schedule

What is necessary to achieve your goals? Making a concrete plan! If you act with purpose and planning in your daily actions, achieving your goals becomes easy!

Here's one example! You can think about what to do during the week, or consider what time of day to exercise. The important thing is to stick to the schedule you set for yourself. What you need to change yourself is the power to keep going.

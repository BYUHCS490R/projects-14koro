Effective Workouts for a Healthier You

This page introduces several effective exercises for weight loss. Even if your goal is to build muscle, you'll find some ideas here for reducing fat!

Plank

Planks are highly effective for both dieting and strength training! Planks allow you to strengthen your core muscles. By strengthening your core muscles, you can expect improved posture maintenance, joint protection, and enhanced metabolism. Strengthening your core muscles is also extremely important for safely performing all the exercises you do! Refference: PubMed Central
